Information to help cope with Depression.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 800-273-8255

Official Website


Depression is a serious issue that many suffer from, all over the world. More is being learned about depression and its effects every day. Regardless of whether you are diagnosed, there are steps that you can take in order to improve your well-being and outlook on life.

Here are some ways to improve your life:
  • SAMHSA's National Helpline: 1-800-662-HELP (4357)
  • Build a social support network. Now more than ever it is important to form strong bonds with those around us. Knowing you can count on a loved one goes a long way towards improving depression.
  • Find a support group. Having others there to support you will make all of the difference. You will know that you aren't alone.

*All information that is provided is based on our collective knowledge as students. If you believe something is seriously wrong, reach out to a professional for help.
