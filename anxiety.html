Information to help cope with Anxiety.

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 800-273-8255


Anxiety is a something that everyone has experienced at some point in their life. Whether it is social anxiety, trauma, or OCD, anxiety impacts many.

Here are some ways to help reduce the effects of anxiety:

  • Breathe. Deep breathing helps to calm down when anxiety is high. Whether you are doing a specific breathing exercise or just focusing on your inhales and exhales, this technique will help to re-center yourself.
  • Seek help. It can never hurt to reach out to others when you suffer from constant anxiety. It is worth it in the end to take the steps needed find peace.
  • Just do something. You will be performing a task that takes your mind off of what is stressful. Regain some control by doing something.

*All information that is provided is based on our collective knowledge as students. If you believe something is seriously wrong, reach out to a professional for help.
